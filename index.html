About Me

I recently graduated from Northwestern University’s Full-Stack Coding Bootcamp where I learned to develop and deploy full stack web applications. I am able to think critically through difficult problems and find a solution given enough time and resources. With an engineering mindset, I want to develop full-stack applications that have a real impact on improving people’s lives.
